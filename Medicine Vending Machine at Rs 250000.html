S S & Associates commenced its business operations in 2005 as Supplier and Retailer to cater the requirements of Vending Machines and VISI Coolers. Our product range includes Premix Based Vending Machines, Sanitary Napkin Vending Machine, Full Line Vending Machine and much more. Our range is highly admired by our clients due to their striking features like hassle-free performance, robust construction, high durability and temperature resistivity. Our product range is made using the high quality raw material, most advanced machinery and under the supervision of skilled professionals. Moreover, we offer customization to our precious clients at market leading prices. Our company has made an impact through 24*7 available Installation and Maintenance Services of Vending Machines. Furthermore, our expertise in efficient machines at competitive prices has helped us in garnering a vast client base.
Being a quality-centric firm, our prime objective is to satisfy our clients by offering them unmatched products in the committed time frame. In order to carry out all our business operations in a hassle free manner, we have set up for ourselves a huge and well established infrastructural set up which is installed with all the latest machines and is divided into multiple departments to maintain efficiency. The departments in which our company is divided include manufacturing unit, warehousing and packaging unit, marketing and sales unit, quality control unit, R & D unit and logistics unit. Working in an effective manner, these departments are headed by their respective managers who guarantee organized and timely completion of tasks in their department. Further the team we have hired endeavors to fulfill assorted needs of our customers in the most satisfactory manner.
